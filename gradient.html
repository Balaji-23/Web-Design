A.P.J ABDUL KALAM IN FULL AVUL PAKIR JAINULABDEEN ABDUL KALAM WAS BORN ON OCTOBER 15 1931, IN RAMESWARAM, TAMILNADU,INDIA.HE SERVED AS THE 11TH PRESIDENT OF INDIA FROM 2002 TO 2007.KALAM EARNED A DEGREE IN AERONAUTICAL ENGINEEERING FROM THE MADRAS INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY AND IN 1958 JOINED THE DEFENCE RESEARCH AND DEVELOPMENT ORGANISATION(DRDO).IN 1969,HE MOVED TO THE INDIAN SPACE RESEARCH ORGANIZATION WHERE HE WAS PROJECT DIRECTOR OF THE SLV-3,THE FIRST SATELLITE LAUNCH VEHICLE THAT WAS BOTH DESIGNED AND PRODUCED IN INDIA.
